Versa Probe Usage (pg4)


Sequence of movements for Outside Corner

Manually jog the probe tip to about 2-10 mm above the workpiece surface.

Manually jog the XY position to about the position indicated by the colored dot on the appropriate button of the Probe Screen.

Set the (edge) length to have the probe move along the edge (away from the corner) the desired distance.

Set the (edge) XY (clearence) to have the probe move away from the edge for probe clearence when lowring the probe in Z .If the XY (clearence) moves the probe farther away then the probe maximum Z travel distance, the probing will fail.

Set the (edge) Z (clearence) to have the probe move down the desired distance for clean material probing.
